yat pūnaretadbhinnavyāvṛttikatayā sattāsiddhāvapi \var{sattā asi° @ra} na kāryatvakṣatirityucyate, tadapi na samyak | vyāvṛttibhede'pi hi sattvasiddhau kṛtakatvasyāpi kathaṃ siddhiḥ \var{sattvasiddhiḥ @ā @ra} ? abhūtvā bhavanaṃ hi kṛtakatvam, bhavanaṃ ca bhavatāmarthakriyākāritvameva | tato'rthakāritvasaṃdehe bhavatītyeva na śakyam | apekṣitaparavyāpāratvaṃ svabhāvaniṣpattau \var{abhāva° @ra} kṛtakatvamityapi nāsato dharmaḥ | sattā cārthakriyaiva | evaṃ pratyayabhede bheditvādayo'pi saddharmā na sattāniścaye'bhidhātuṃ śakyanta iti |

atrocyate | na tāvadaṅkurādayaḥ parito nirastavibhramāśaṅke'bhyāsaviśoṣāsāditapaṭimni \var{sādi paṭi° @a} pratyakṣacetasi parisphuranto na santaḥ \var{no'santaḥ @ra} śākyante'bhidhātumadarśane'pi phalasya \var{halasya @a} | anyathā arthakriyāyāmapi na kaścidāśvāsa iti saditaravyavahāravirahānnirīhaṃ jagajjāyeta |

atha sattvalakṣaṇaṃ tāvad \var{tāvaditi nāsti @ra} bhavatāmarthakriyāsāmarthyameveti cet ? tat kimidānīṃ samayasiddhena lakṣaṇena pratyakṣabādho'bhidheyaḥ \var{°dhobidheyaḥ @ra} ? tad yadi bhavato'pi lakṣaṇadoṣa evānena krameṇa vaktumiṣṭo'bhidhīyatām | asmākaṃ tu na \var{neti nāsti @a} tallakṣaṇaprasādhanāya parikarabandhaḥ \var{sādhanopapari° @ra}, kiṃ tu tallakṣaṇena \var{°kṣaṇe @a} kṣaṇabhaṅgasādhanāya | atastatlakṣaṇānavadhāraṇaṃ kṣaṇabhaṅgasiddhipratibandhamādadhīta kārye | kāryāntaradarśane tu tatrāpi tadanumānaprasavaḥ krameṇa kena vāryate ? idānīṃ tvarthakriyāsamarthasyaiva pakṣīkaraṇāt pakṣabahirbhūte sādhyasādhanayorasiddhāvapi na kaścid doṣa itayuktaprāyam \var{prāyaḥ @a} |

tasmāt sāmayikalakṣaṇakṣatāvapi kiṃ punaraniścayamātre paṭutaraprtryakṣabalāvalambitavastubhāve \var{°tāvastu° @ra} aṅkurādivasnuni vastudharmasya kāryatvasya virodha \var{naṃ varodha @ā} iti | tadapekṣayā bījādikamarthakriyāsāmarthyasamarthanasahameva \var{°sāmarthanasahameva @ā} , kathamasiddhiḥ ?

nanvarthakriyāsāmarthyamitaradvā \var{°matarad @ra} vastulakṣaṇamaspṛśatendriyajñānena kathaṃ vastutvamupasthāpitamityucyate \var{°avasthāyitaṃ @ā} ? tadapi yat kiñcit , anubhave pramāṇāntaracintāyā niravakāśatvāt | dṛśyate hi śāstrīyalakṣaṇānullekhepyabhyāsabhāvitapratyakṣodayamātrād \var{°bharita° @ā} vastutvamavasāya pravṛttiravisaṃvādabhāginīti kimatra kriyatām \var{kriyātā @a} ? evaṃ tu syāt | yadi paramārthato'rthakriyāsāmarthyameva vastutvaṃ tadā idamato jāyata iti janmādhiraṇaṃ vastūllikhatā \var{°dhyeṣaṇārtha° @ra @ā} adhyakṣeṇārthakriyāsāmarthyamevollikhitam | athānyadeva vastulakṣaṇaṃ tadā tadevollikhitam | yathā \var{°ṇīmayatva @ā @ra}triguṇamayatvamitaradvā'nullikhitā tadabahirbhūtaṃ nīlaṃ nīlagrāhiṇā \var{°meva tatsi° @a, mevā @ra}gṛhītamevātatsiddhamadhyakṣeṇa vastutvamaṅkurā[2b]deḥ\var{nastattamaṅkurādeḥ @ra} kāryatāsaṃvāde'pi\var{kāryatāmasvāmi @a} kāryāntarādarśane'pi kāraṇasya

bojāderarthasāmarthyamavasthāpayatīti | kāryavat tarhi bījādikamapi pratyakṣodayādevāstu vastusat\var{prastuptaṃ° @a, vastukimartho @ra} , kimarthasāmarthyagaveṣaṇeneti cet na vai vastuvyavahāre pratyakṣapāṭavaṃ paribhūyārthaśaktimanusarāmaḥ , tasyā api pratyakṣādhīnatvāt | kri tu kṣaṇabhaṅgamaṇgīkārayitum | pratyakṣakṛto hi vastuvyavahāra uktakrameṇa tadatallakṣaṇasādhāraṇo na kṣaṇikatvasādhanāya\var{sādhanīya @ra} niyamena prabhavati | arthakriyāsāmarthyaṃ tu vastulakṣaṇamamabhavadapi yatra svayaṃ vartate, tatrāvaśyaṃ tadanupanīya na \var{svarūpatāmā° @ra}svarūpasattāmāviśati | tataḥ \var{sādhanake @ra}sādhanatvenāsya prastāvo'tra na vastulakṣaṇatveneti sthitam ||

athārthaśakteḥ sarvathā sūkṣmavicārāsahatvād a[rtha]śaktaṃ sarvamiti kva pakṣīkaraṇam , kiṃ vā sādhanamiti cet - nanu bījāderaṅkurādiṣu dṛṣṭā śaktiḥ , nahi dṛṣṭe anupapannaṃ nāma | sarvasya hi vicāranāmnaḥ pramāṇasyānubhava eva sīmā | kiṃ caivamanicchan prāmāṇikaḥ kathaṃ dhūmādapi dahanamanumāsyati ? jalajvalanayorvātātapaśītātaḥ sadṛśīṃ pravṛttimālambeta , apravṛttiṃ vā | bhavāneva kathaṃ vā mithyāśāktimāropayatā pareṇa sādhana upanyaste tadabhāvaṃ | vidvānevaitatparājayāśaṃsī dūṣaṇopanyāsottarito'sti ? tatrāpyasiddhyādikameva vaktumādriyase , na tu jātyuttaram\var{jātyuttaraḥ @a} ? tadetadāyātam , nātmanāpi pratyaya iti | tasmād brūyāḥ kiñcit kenacit pratāritaḥ | durapahnavā tu śaktirartheṣu | sābhāsā cet - saṃvṛttyā , na paramārthena | astu yathā tathā saṃvṛttyā paramārthena vā, kimatra nirbandhena ? |
