	[297] evaṃ sati saty api vyāptir aśakyaniścayā saṃbhāvyata iti kim atra sāmpratam iti cet? paśya,
	kāryaṃ tāvad anekajāti, janakaścaiko jaṭo vīkṣyate nānābhedavato na caikaniyamajñāne 'nyabhede 'pi tat |
	yadyajjātikam ekahetuviditaṃ tattattadaṅgaṃ tato naivaṃ ced atisañjanaṃ na ca mataḥ sādhyopame 'yannayaḥ || na hi nānāprakārakāryajananaṃ nāma prayatnavaty eva niyatam, vahnyāder api vividhasāmagrīmadhyamadhyāsīnasya dhūmabhasmāṅgārāṅgatāpapākapāratsāraṇavīkṣaṇakṣatiprabhṛtivicitrakāryakriyādarśanāt (dhūmabhasmāṅgārāṅgatāpapākapāradotsāraṇavīkṣaṇakṣatiprabhṛtivicitrakāryakriyādarśanāt) | yadi paraṃ prayatnavato 'dhiṣṭhānenety atra vivādaḥ, na caikasya tatra niyame jñāte kāryāntare 'pi niyamajñānam upapadyate, yathoktaṃ prāk | tato yāvantaḥ prakārā ekahetavo niścitāḥ, tāvantas tasya hetoścetanasyetarasya vā sādhanaṃ yujyante | anyathā stimitendhanasakhena śikhinā gṛhītadhūmavyāptir aparicitatatkāryabhāvair bhasmādibhir api dahanam anuminuyāt | na caivaṃ, tasmāt tajjātibhedo vyakti vyāptigrahaś ca pṛthak pṛthagapekṣyamāṇo yatra kvacit tadgrahaṇena yena kenacid anumānam unmūlayati | kaḥ punar evam āha, yatra kvacid gṛhītavyāptir yena kenacid anumimītām iti, ekadharmānuvṛttivirahe hetor evābhāvāt? na hi tadā bhasmasādhanam avyāpteḥ | nāpi dhūmo 'siddheḥ | kuṭakūṭādau kāryatvam evānyadharmānanuvṛttāv apy anumāne vartamānaṃ hetuḥ syād iti cet, na, kāryatvasyāpi prāgavyāpter vistareṇa sādhanāt | tatra yathā kulagiriḥ kulālavyāpārakāle svabhāvavaikalyādikalitaniyamah, tathā sad api kāryatvaṃ prāmāṇyapravṛttyā dhūmajanmanīva rāsabha iti pramāṇena vyāptigrahavirahe na kaścid viśeṣaḥ | tad ayaṃ ghaṭādau kāryaviśeṣe gṛhītavyāptiḥ sarvato na kāryatāmātreṇa vā vyāpakam anumātu | na tu vyāptigrahaṇasādhanavyavasthayor bhinnādhikaraṇatām atikrāmatīti katham atiprasaṅgopālambhaparihāraḥ? 
tasmān naikadharmānuvṛttimātreṇopayogaḥ | kena vā dhūmasaṃbhavinaḥ kāryatvavastutvādayo bhasmani bhasmasād eva kṛtāḥ? santo 'pi vyabhicāriṇa iti cet? nanu savyabhicāro vyāptiviyogād eva, vyāptau tadgandhasyābhāvāt | sa ca vyāpty abhāvaḥ kāryatvenāpi arjita (avarjita) iti tatrāpi vyabhicāro durnivāraḥ | āhaty ādarśanaṃ tu viprakarṣād api syāt | 
	[298] na tāvatā tadabhāva eva, tanmūlabhūtāyā avyāptes tādavasthyāt | yadi tu vyāptāv eva savyabhicāraḥ, tadā nitarāmavyāptāv ity alaṃ mithyābhiniveśena | tad yady api sādhāraṇadharmā tanmātraṃ vyāptim anādṛtya sādhanam iṣyate, kiṃ na bhinna evaṃ bhūdharādisādhāraṇaṃ dharmam, anādṛtyety atiprasaṅgas tadavasthaḥ? 
nāpy ayaṃ nayaḥ sādhyasamānīkṛte kalasādāv apy asti, tatrāpi yathāyathā pratisaraśarābodañcanādiṣu kumbhakārakṛtim upalabhāmahe, tathātathaivam apīdam apy anena sujyata iti pratiyantaḥ paricitakriyebhya eva prakārebhyas tadvyāpāram avadhārayāmaḥ, anyathā taddoṣānativṛttiḥ | tasmād yadi paramārthataḥ prayatnavannāntarīyaka evāyaṃ sakalaprakāraḥ kāryagrāmaḥ, tathāpi svayam akiñcitkara iti pramāṇavṛttyapekṣayā, 

	siddhaṃ yādṛg adhiṣṭhātṛbhāvābhāvānuvṛttimat |
	sanniveśādi tadyuktaṃ tasmād yad anumīyate || (PV II 13)

iti || yaddṛṣṭer akriyādarśino 'pi kṛtabuddhir bhavati, śūnyanagaraparidṛṣṭadevakulādivat sanniveśādi,

	tādṛg eva yadīkṣyeta tannagādiṣu dharmiṣu |
	yuktaṃ tatsādhanād asmād yathābhīṣṭasya sādhanam ||

tac cāsiddhim ity ākūtam | 
nanu bhavaty evāsmākaṃ sarvatra pauruṣeyabuddhiḥ, kṛtabuddhiḥ sā, sādhyabuddhir evānumānam iti niyamaḥ, kathañcit kasyacit tatrāpy abhāvāt | kiṃ ca kṛtabuddhir iti kāryamātrabuddhir vā, puruṣamātrabuddhir vā? yadi kṛtamātrabuddhiḥ, sā bauddhasyāpi siddhā sindhubhūdharādiṣu | sāvayavatvāc ca tadārambhasaṃbhavitvam eva kṛtatvam | atha pauruṣeyabuddhiḥ kṛtabuddhiḥ? sā sādhyabuddhir evānumānam iti cocyate, tatas tadabhāvān nānumānam iti katham ucyamānaṃ nopaplavate ? 
yadāha, na ca vyāmohāt kasyacit sādhyabuddhyabhāve hetor asiddhatādoṣaḥ, sarvānumāneṣu tadbhāvaprasaṅgād iti | yad apīdam ucyate, tādṛkkāryatvādi niṣiddham acalādiṣv iti, 
	[299] tatrāpi na kāryatvādinā kailāsakalasādeḥ kiñcidviśeṣam upalabhāmahe | āha ca, nāpi kaścid viśeṣaḥ kṣityādihetūnāṃ prāsādādihetubhyo yenāmī nirabhiprāyāḥ santaḥ prayatnanirapekṣāḥ pratiniyamavatā ca vyāpāreṇa tadvantaḥ syuḥ, na hy anadhiṣṭhitānāṃ prayatnavatā karaṇadharmāṇāṃ kriyāsu svātantryam upalabdhipūrvam, sarvatra buddhimadadhiṣṭhiteṣv eva prāsādādiprasavopalabdher iti |
atrocyate, neyaṃ kṛtabuddhiḥ siddhāntābhiniveśavāsitabuddhīn adhikṛtya kīrtitā, kiṃ tu saṃvyavahārapragalbhapuruṣamātrāpekṣayā | tasya ca pūrvapratītapratibandhasmaraṇavataḥ kāraṇaparāmarśavaimukhyābhāve bhavaty eva devakulādau tādṛśī buddhir na tu malyamakarālyādau | tad anayor asyā dhiyo bhāvābhāvau na jātibhedam avyavasthāpya sthātuṃ prabhavataḥ, dhūmadvayadarśane 'pi tathābhāvaprasaṅgāt | iyaṃ ca kāmaṃ sādhyabuddhir eva, kaḥ punar enām etadvato 'numānanibandhanam āha, tadabhāvād vā bhūdharādau tadabhavam? kiṃ tu jātibhedamātropalakṣaṇārtham asyā bhāvābhāvopanyāsaḥ samānajanāpekṣayā kasyacid durvidagdhabuddheḥ pratibodhasādhanāya | jātibhede hi tāvati siddhe naikatra pravṛttim adhyakṣānupalambham anyatra pratibandhasādhanasamartham iti vaktuṃ samarthaḥ syāt |
tato nāpratibaddhāt sanniveśādeḥ sādhyasiddhiḥ, pratibaddhaś cāsiddha iti katham anumānam ity antato 'numānaniṣedhāyopanyāse 'pi naikasyaiva tadabhāvāt tadabhāva ity uktaṃ bhavati | nanu tathāpy anyasyānumānodayābhāvād anyasyāpi na bhavitavyam ity uktaṃ bhavati | tac ca kathaṃ saṅgatimālambeta? uktaṃ hi, na ca vyāmohād ity ādi | tad ayuktam | samānasādhanasāmagrīsaṃbhave sādhyasyaikapratiniyamānupapatteḥ | yad yena viśiṣṭasāmagrīkaṃ tat tenāviśiṣṭaphalam eva, yathā sādhāraṇadhūmasādhanendhanadahanādisamudāyam pradeśadvayam anyonyam, aviśiṣṭānumānasādhanapratibandhagrahaṇādisāmagrīkaś ca bhavatā saha sāṃvyavahārikaḥ puruṣa iti svabhāvahetuḥ prasaṅgaḥ | bhavato 'pi hi pratyakṣānupalambham eva pramāṇaṃ pratibandhasādhanaṃ devakulasūtradhārādyadhikāreṇa pravṛttam | etac ca parasyāpi sādhāraṇam | asādhāraṇaṃ tu viparyayabādhakapramāṇam eva durabhūyatayā bhavet | tac ca bhavato 'pi nāstīti prāk prakāśitam |
tad yadi svamatābhiniveśamātram anubhavato nāvṛṇoti cetaḥ, kiṃ tu tad eva sādhanam itarasādhāraṇam, tadā tasyāpi śūnyanagaraparidṛṣṭadevakulādivan merumandarādāv api pauruṣeyānumānam anivāryam | na caivam | tato yasya yatpratibandhagrāhakapramāṇavyāpāre 
[300] yathāvat smaraṇavato yaddarśanān na tadanumānaṃ na tasya yatra pratibandhaḥ siddho, yathāgnau dhūmapratibandhasyānusmarato gardabhadarśanād agniman anuminvataḥ | na bhavati ca cetanena devakulādipratibandhasmṛtimato malayahimālayādau puruṣānumānam iti kāryānupalabdhiḥ smaraṇakāraṇānusaraṇavaimukhyādipratyayāntarasaṃbhave prācīnapratibandhasādhakapramāṇena viṣayīkaraṇavaikalyaṃ sādhayati | tac ca jātibhedātiśayam antareṇāvāntarabhedavato 'pi, dhūmād gardabhavat |
vyāptigrahaṇasmaraṇayor aparādhe tu neyam iṣṭasiddhiḥ | tadarthaṃ yathāvad grahaṇam | tatra smaraṇāparādhe devakulāntarād api nānumimīta, grahaṇāparādho 'pi pratyakṣāpekṣayā 'saṃbhāvī, ekapratyavamarśayogyatāyā evābhāvād ity ukteḥ | anyathā jaladharādyarthino 'pi kumbhakārasūtradhārādīnapekṣeran | apekṣata eva tatrāpi sūtradhārasthānīyo 'nyo yathā kumbhakārasthānīyaḥ kvacit sūtradhāra iti cet, na, kāryajātivaisadṛśyābhāve kāraṇasāmarthyavaisadṛśyāpekṣānupapatteḥ | anyathā kumbhāntare 'pi na kulālamātreṇa toṣam aśnuvīta | tadvad vā kṛṣīvalaḥ kulālapauruṣāpaneyānāvṛṣṭikaṣṭaḥ syād iti siddha eva tādṛśo jātibhedaḥ, tasmād ekākārapratyavamarśāśnupapatter adhyakṣasādhyā vyāptir viśiṣṭaviṣayatayā na durgrahadoṣam āvahati, yato 'numānābhāve 'pi jātibhedātiśayasyāsiddheḥ siddhasādhanadoṣaḥ syāt |
tataś ca yathāvad grahaṇasiddher yatra nānumānaṃ tatra vyāpter evāgrahād iti nānekāntaḥ | kathaṃ punar anumānahānam eva tādṛśi lokasyāgantavyam (lokasyāvagantavyam) iti cet? uktād eva jaladharārthino 'pi kulālajaṅghāmardanaprasaṅgāt, paracakrākrāntikātarasya vā kulagirijaladhivyavadhim abhilaṣataḥ, cetanamātreṇa vyāptiṃ pratīmo no kumbhakārādyavasthāniyatena tena nāyaṃ prasaṅga iti cet? tadavasthāniyatenaiva vyāptigrahe kīdṛśo doṣa iti kim atra vaktavyam? na ca samayāpakṣālakalāṅkitadhiyaṃ bhavantaṃ prati kācic cintā, api tu lokaṃ prati | so 'pi vyabhicārān na tena pratipadyate iti cet? evaṃ tarhi kalasāntarād api nānumānam ayaṃ kuryāt |
anyatraiva vyabhicāradarśanād atra ko doṣa iti cet? tad etaj jātivaisadṛśye 'sati na labhyate | tataś ca viśiṣṭāyām eva jātau vyāptigrāhi pratyakṣaṃ pravṛttam ato nānyatra tadvyāpārānumānam ity evaṃ kiṃ nocyate, kim atra vyabhicāropakṣepeṇa? avaśyaṃ ca pratyakṣasya pratiniyatajātivyavasāya eṣitavyo 'nyathā vyabhicārāgocaram api pratyakṣaṃ na niyatajātikam iti kalasāntaram apy āskandet | tathā ca sati sa eva doṣaḥ | vyāptigrāhiṇaiva 
[301] kalasāntarasyādhiṣṭhitatvād iti cet? nanu tadadhiṣṭhita eva viṣaye vyabhicāracāri cetaḥ pracarat kathaṃ tam eva pariharet? 
ubhayor niyataviṣayatve tu nāyaṃ doṣaḥ | kevalam idānīm adhyakṣeṇa viśiṣṭaviṣayasvīkārān na vyabhicāropanyāsaḥ saphalaḥ | vyāptigrāhiṇas tu pramāṇasya viśiṣṭaviṣayapravṛttismaraṇādhānārtham upayujyante mūṭhaṃ prati | na tu parito visāriṇī vyāptigṛhītarasya niyataviṣayā kriyate vyabhicāreṇa | nāpy anumānakhaṇṭanamātram uktanyāyād eva | tasmād viśiṣṭaviṣayaiva vyāptiḥ kumbhakārādinā gṛhyate vyabhicāradarśanādarśanāpekṣyapratyakṣasyaikajātyapekṣayaiva pravṛtter iti siddham |
evaṃ cetanamātreṇāpi vyāptijighṛkṣāyāṃ vyabhicāro darśanābhāvāt yajjātini vastuni pratyakṣapravṛttis tad eva tena vyāptam sidhyatīty api siddham | tat kathaṃ sāmānyena vyāptim abhidadhānaḥ samayānuvādamātreṇa nopālambham arhati? yadi tu puruṣamātreṇa vyāptiṃ jighṛkṣor adhyakṣapratikṣepeṇa pramāṇāntaraṃ śaraṇam, tadā naiṣa doṣaḥ | tat tu vicāritam | yadi veśvaraprasādād asya vādino jagataś citrākāraparihāreṇa sarvatraikākāraṃ pratyakṣam udayam āsādayati, taccāsādhāraṇam iti na vyavahārāṅgam | saśarīrāśarīrayoḥ prayatnavator na kaścid viśeṣaḥ karmadharmāpekṣayā pratyakṣeṇa vyāptigrahaṇe, yato jaladharārthinā kumbhakārānanusaraṇena tatkṛtānumānavirahānumāne prakārāntareṇāpi saṃbhāvanāyāṃ jātibhedaikanibandhanāpratibandhāt siddhasādhanaṃ punar apy āsajyeta | tato jātibhedakṛtas tatrānumānābhāvaḥ saṃsidhyan pratiniyatajātipratibaddham anumānam upapādayati |
viśiṣṭajātipratibaddhapauruṣeyānumānāsiddhau vā anubhavasiddham api jātibhedaṃ śāṭhyād apahnuvānaḥ paralokasya jātibhedānantarīyakeṇa tathā pravṛttyabhāvasiddhena kulālādikṛtatvānumānābhāvena | anyathānumānodayaṃ prasajya jātibhedam āpādyate, yathā sthāṇukākābhyāṃ bhinnaḥ saṃyogo nāma yadi syād dṛśyo loko 'py enaṃ tṛtīyam īkṣeteti ucyate | tathā ca yadi yādṛśaḥ sanniveśo buddhimatpūrvako dṛṣṭas tādṛg eva jaladharādāv api syāt, lokasyāpi puruṣakṛtabuddhiḥ syāt, ghaṭāntaravat |
na caivaṃ tajjātibhedakṛto 'yaṃ vibhāgo 'nyanimittābhāvād ity uktaṃ bhavati | tataḥ kīdṛśaṃ punaḥ sanniveśādyadhiṣṭhātṛanāntarīyakaṃ siddham ity apekṣāyāṃ yāddarśanād akriyādarśinaḥ apīty ādy ucyate, paryāyaparikalpitānekaprakāraparigaṇanagauravaparihārārtham, tatra puruṣakṛtabuddhir iti prayatnavad anumānaṃ vā kumbhakārādivyāpārānumānaṃ vety ubhayasyāpi saṅgateḥ 
	[302] prāguktakramāt | kevalaṃ prayatnavanmātraniṣedhasādhanasāmagrīkatayā kulālānumānābhāva eva liṅgam, tadvitsiddho vā vyāptigrahaṇābhāvaḥ | etac ca parecchām anulāpya pallavitam |
lokas tu yathādarśanānusārī yajjātikaṃ puruṣapūrvakaṃ paśyati, tattattādṛśā tanumataiva kṛtam avabudhyate, na tu śarīraviratisandeham apy ullikhatīti samānamānavānumānaparyavasānaiva kṛtabuddhir avatiṣṭhata iti | yadāpi yadi kasyacit siddhivaśād āsurīṃ māyālim ālocayato 'cale 'pi kaścid ullekho jāyate, kenāpi kair api vā kaccit kṛto 'yam api bhaved iti, tadāpi kenāpi | karacaraṇāṅgoparakta evātyantaṃ pratibhāti | niścayas tu sudūra eva | na caikajātitve tadupapadyate, dhūmakumbhāder ivānalakulālādeḥ | na ca karacaraṇādivyāpāravirahiṇa ullekho 'pi | na ca tāvatā kṣaṇikādivādino 'numānāpalāpaḥ śakyate | adhyakṣapūrvavyāptigrahasahāyaṃ tu jātiviśeṣāpekṣavṛttikam anumānam ubhayor api sādhāraṇatvān na saṃbhavati laukikaparīkṣayor iti brūmaḥ | yady api ca dvitīyam api laukikam eva, tathāpi kaścid viṣayo 'nabhyastatayā tīvratarābhyūhasāpekṣaḥ kṣmadhiyaḥ (sūkṣmadhiyaṛ) sāṃvyavahārikasyāpi saṃbhavati | tatra pratyakṣākṣamatve 'pi vipakṣabādhakenānumānāntareṇa lokapratītim evāśritya gṛhītā vyāptir anumānaṃ prayojayed iti na kṣatiḥ, yathā yatrārthāpattighoṣaṇāviśeṣo lokasya | tasmāt sādhyabuddhirūpā kṛtabuddhir bhāvābhāvābhyāṃ jātibhedaṃ prasādhayatīti yuktam eva | yadāpi kṛtamātrabuddhiḥ kṛtabuddhiḥ, tadāpi na doṣaḥ | tathāpi kṛtakatvaṃ nāma svajanmany apekṣitaparavyāpāratvam | tac ca na tāvad vṛkṣādivat pratyakṣataḥ kṣitidharādiṣu siddham | parābhyupagamamātrasiddhaṃ tu svatantrasādhanādhikāre 'naṅgam eva |
tato 'numānasiddham āśrīyamāṇaṃ dṛṣṭasajātīyaṃ yathā kathañcid vāśrīyate? anantyapakṣe yady anādyupādānaśaktisaṃtanyamānakṣaṇaparamparārūpam eva kāryatvaṃ niyatadeśatayā vyavasthāpyamānaṃ sidhyet, tādṛśāt, kīdṛśī puruṣādhiṣṭhānapratyāśā? dṛṣṭasajātīyaṃ tu tadaṅgam ucitam | cetanācetanādhiṣṭhānakṛtaṃ viśeṣam avadhūya vitatānekakāraṇakalāpamilanalabhyodayaṃ hi puruṣavyāpāram ākarṣed api, milanasya tadadhīnādhyavasāyāt | acetanopādānatvamātram api hi prācīnakrameṇa caritārthaṃ na prakṛtaṃ viśeṣam ākarṣet | etad eva tu kāryatvaṃ loke viditam | tac ca nācalādau siddham iti katham asiddhiparihāraḥ? kevalaṃ na 
[303] sākṣād asiddhisādhanam idam, kiṃ tu vijātīyatvasādhanārtham | na ca kāryatvamātram adhikṛtya, kiṃ tu sarvam |
tad ayam arthaḥ, kiṃ tadadhiṣṭhātṛbhāvābhāvānuvṛttisanniveśādisiddham, yaddarśanād akriyādarśino 'pi lokasya kṛtabuddhir bhavatīti | tato yadi sāvayavā kṛtabuddhir api bhavato bhavati, tathāpi lokasya nāstīti bhinnajātīyatvaṃ vyavasthāpyate | anyathā kumbhakārādināpi bhūdharādivyāpyaṃ pratīyāl loka iti pūrvavat prasaṅgaḥ | tato jātibhedād anyad eva pratyakṣānupalambhābhyāṃ puruṣakṛtaṃ pratītam iti siddham |
yat tūktaṃ kāryatve na kaścid viśeṣa iti | atra sarvasādhāraṇadharmatve 'pi kāryatvasya yadi pratyakṣasādhanā vyāptir abhimatā, tadā sad api tadudāsīnam evety uktaprāyam | yadi tu viśeṣam utsṛjyābhūtvābhāvamātrasya cetanāvyāptir vivakṣitā, tadā pratyakṣavyāpāram apy utsṛjya pramāṇāntaraṃ śaraṇam astv ity api | tadabhāve vā na kiñcit kāryatvena satāpi |
